How to Make Tips to Eating Soy sauce

In order to enjoy sushi, you must first learn to make it. One of the most common ways to Prepare sushi is in "rolls". There are 9 simple steps to making a roll.

  • Cook your rice
  • Prepare fish and anything else you want inside of your roll
  • Put Nori(seaweed) on bamboo mat, with the shinier side down
  • Add rice vinegar to rice
  • Wet your hands, spread rice on nori leaving a small size gap(1 inch or so) without rice at the top
  • Place chosen ingredients in center of rice
  • Roll the sushi with the bamboo mat
  • Wet your knife, cut roll into desired size
  • Eat and thank me

This includes wasabi, the Japanese form of horseradish. Wasabi is a spicy ingredient that adds a nice flavor to your sushi. Another yummy addition is soy sauce. Soy sauce is a salty sauce that adds a nice taste to the fish when added in moderation. To properly eat sushi with soy sauce, it is recommended that you dip only the fish in, rather than both the fish and the rice.
